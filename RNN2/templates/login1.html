About Us

This is our Undergraduate Final Year Project on Stock Market Forecasting. The goal of the stock market forecasting project is to create a model that can estimate future stock values using historical data and pertinent parameters. The ultimate objective is to give relevant insights and predictions to investors and traders so that they may make educated stock market decisions. The project combines data analysis, machine learning, and financial knowledge to provide a trustworthy and effective tool for forecasting stock market trends and prices.
